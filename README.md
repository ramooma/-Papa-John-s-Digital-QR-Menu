# 🍕 Papa John's Digital QR Menu

### A responsive digital menu built to enhance customer experience and ordering efficiency.

## 📌 The Problem
During my time working at a Papa John's branch, I noticed that the physical menus provided to customers were too small. This caused frustration and significantly delayed the ordering process, affecting the overall workflow during rush hours.

## 💡 The Solution
I designed and developed a digital menu website accessible via a **QR code** placed on every table. This allowed customers to comfortably browse the menu on their phones. 

**Key Highlights:**
- 📸 **Custom Photography:** I personally took high-quality photos of the pizzas and items to make the menu more appealing.
- 📱 **Mobile-First Design:** Optimized specifically for smartphones since customers access it via QR codes.
- 🚀 **Improved Efficiency:** Drastically reduced ordering time and improved customer satisfaction.

## 🛠️ Built With
- **HTML5:** For structuring the menu items and categories.
- **CSS3:** For a clean, appetizing, and responsive layout.
